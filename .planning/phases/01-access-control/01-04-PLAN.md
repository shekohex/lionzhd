  <files>routes/web.php</files>
  <action>
Update `routes/web.php` to enforce role boundaries via middleware:

Apply `can:server-download` (Admin-only per Plan 01-02) to:
- `movies.download` (GET /movies/{model}/download)
- `series.download.single` (GET /series/{model}/{season}/{episode}/download)
- `series.download.batch` (POST /series/{model}/download)

Apply `can:download-operations` (Admin-only) to:
- `downloads.edit` (PATCH /downloads/{model})
- `downloads.destroy` (DELETE /downloads/{model})

Do NOT restrict direct-download routes (must remain usable by External members):
- `movies.direct`
- `series.direct.single`
- `series.direct.batch`

Expected behavior:
- External member receives 403 (Inertia 403 UX where applicable)
- Members receive 403 for download operations routes
- Admin is allowed through middleware
  </action>
  <verify>
